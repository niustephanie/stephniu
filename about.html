Stephanie Niu is a poet and data storyteller from Marietta, Georgia. She is the author of Survived By, winner of the 2023 Host Publications Chapbook Prize, and She Has Dreamt Again of Water, winner of the 2021 Diode Chapbook Contest.

She received her degrees in symbolic systems and computer science from Stanford University. Her work has appeared in Copper Nickel, Waxwing, Ecotone, The Georgia Review, and elsewhere. She is currently completing a Fulbright Study/Research Award for digital heritage work on immigration and labor history on Christmas Island, an Australian territory in the Indian Ocean.
