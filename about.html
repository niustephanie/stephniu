Stephanie Niu is a digital storyteller and poet who lives in the San Francisco Bay Area. Her work focuses on understanding ecological systems and decolonizing historical narratives through digital techniques, including podcast production, map-making, and digital visualization. She is the receipient of a Fulbright Study/Research Award, a Levinthal tutorial in poetry, and the Beagle II Award for independent research.
