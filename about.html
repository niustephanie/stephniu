Stephanie Niu is a digital storyteller and poet who lives in northern California. Her work focuses on understanding ecological systems and decolonizing historical narratives through digital techniques, including podcast production, map-making, and digital visualization. She is the receipient of a Fulbright Study/Research Award, a Levinthal tutorial in poetry, and the Beagle II Award for independent research. She currently works as a product manager at Datadog.
