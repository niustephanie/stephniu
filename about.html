Stephanie Niu is a poet, writer, and digital humanities scholar from Marietta, Georgia. She is the author of Survived By: An Atlas of Disappearance, winner of the 2023 Host Publications Chapbook Prize, and She Has Dreamt Again of Water, winner of the 2021 Diode Editions Chapbook Contest. Her work has appeared in The Georgia Review, Lit Hub, The Missouri Review, Copper Nickel, The Offing, and elsewhere.
