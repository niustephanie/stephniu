Stephanie Niu is a poet and writer from Marietta, Georgia. Her first full-length poetry collection, I Would Define the Sun, won the inaugural Vanderbilt University Literary Prize and is forthcoming in February 2025. She is the author of the chapbooks Survived By: An Atlas of Disappearance, winner of the 2023 Host Publications Chapbook Prize, and She Has Dreamt Again of Water, winner of the 2021 Diode Editions Chapbook Contest. Her work has appeared in The Georgia Review, The Missouri Review, Literary Hub, Copper Nickel, Ecotone, and elsewhere.
